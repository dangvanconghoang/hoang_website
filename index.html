Research Interests

Causal Inference

Developing and applying causal inference methods to understand cause-and-effect relationships in complex, observational data.

Causal Discovery

Learning causal structures from data using constraint-based and score-based algorithms to uncover hidden mechanisms.

Treatment Effect Estimation

Estimating causal effects of interventions using methods such as propensity scoring, instrumental variables, and doubly robust estimators.

Causal Machine Learning

Integrating causal reasoning with machine learning to build models that generalize beyond correlations.
